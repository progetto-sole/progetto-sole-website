Sole Studio is founded by Asia Trianda and Nicolò Cozza. At Sole, we believe ideas—and the design process that follows—should emerge from a place of tranquillity and calm, rather than the hyper-productivity of the fast-paced environments we’ve grown accustomed to today. We find this mindset under the sun. 

Our focus is on cultivating meaningful design by exploring how material and visual experiences can form new narratives on a spatial level. Our Services:

Exhibition & Spatial Design
Event Design & Organization
Set Design & Set-Up
Event Production & Management

Creative Direction
Graphic & Digital Design
